# Product Requirement Document (PRD): AGRILOG

**Product Name:** AGRILOG  
**Document Version:** 1.0  
**Target Audience:** Farmers, Equipment Rental Partners, Domestic & Enterprise/International Crop Buyers  

---

## 1. Product Overview

**AGRILOG** is an end-to-end, multilingual agricultural ecosystem designed to support farmers throughout their entire farming lifecycle. By integrating **crop planning (demand forecasting)**, **farm equipment rentals/purchases**, and **verified direct market access**, AGRILOG eliminates fragmentation in the agricultural supply chain to maximize farmer profitability and yield efficiency.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Indian farmers spend up to 6 months per crop cycle investing significant time, capital, and labor without visibility into market demand or final selling prices. The primary pain points include:

1. **Information Asymmetry:** Lack of data on actual market demand, leading to overproduction of lower-margin crops.
2. **Resource Constraints:** Smallholder farmers lack direct, affordable access to modern agricultural machinery and inputs.
3. **Price Realization & Intermediaries:** After harvest, farmers struggle to connect directly with trustworthy, high-paying buyers, resulting in distress sales at depressed prices.

---

## 3. Product Vision & Unique Selling Proposition (USP)

### Vision
To create sustainable, scalable growth for farmers by transforming traditional farming into a demand-driven, technology-enabled ecosystem.

### USP
While existing platforms address isolated legs of the supply chain, **AGRILOG combines crop planning, equipment access, and end-to-end trading on a single, multilingual platform** that accompanies the farmer from pre-sowing to post-harvest sales.

## 5. Key Feature Specifications & User Journey

```
[ Pre-Sowing ] ──────► [ Cultivation ] ──────► [ Post-Harvest ]
  Crop Planning          Equipment Rental        Direct Buyer Sales
  & Insights             & Input Marketplace     & Negotiation Support
```

### Module 1: Demand-Based Crop Advisory Engine (Pre-Sowing)
* **Market Demand Matching:** Recommends optimal crops based on real-time market demand and buyer contracts rather than traditional guesswork.
* **Multilingual Interface:** Vernacular language support (Hindi, regional dialects, voice-assisted input) to ensure accessibility for low-literacy users.

### Module 2: Equipment & Resource Hub (Sowing / Cultivation)
* **Rental & Purchase Marketplace:** Enables farmers to browse, rent, or purchase agricultural machinery locally via partnered equipment owners.
* **Partner Portal:** Simple onboarding interface for equipment suppliers to list assets and manage bookings.

### Module 3: Verified Buyer Marketplace & Trade Facilitation (Post-Harvest)
* **Buyer Matchmaking:** Connects farmers with verified domestic and international buyers once crops approach harvest.
* **B2B Coordination & Negotiation:** Platform-assisted negotiation tools to ensure fair price discovery and contract clarity.
* **Logistics & Trade Support:** Operational assistance for buyer coordination and dispatch.

## 7. Business & Monetization Model

AGRILOG utilizes a multi-stream revenue architecture designed to scale with user growth:

1. **Transaction Commission:** Percentage fee charged on successful trades executed between farmers and buyers.
2. **Equipment Partnerships:** Revenue-share model on equipment rentals and machinery transactions completed through the platform.
3. **Enterprise Subscriptions & Premium Advisory:** Paid subscription tiers for enterprise buyers seeking bulk sourcing feeds and advanced advisory tools for large farm groups.

* **Phase 2 (Feedback & Validation):** Refine UI/UX based on transaction metrics, user retention, and feedback from ground operations.
* **Phase 3 (Global Expansion):** Scale verified buyer channels to include international traders, linking Indian produce directly to global demand.

---

## 9. Key Success Metrics (KPIs)

* **GMV (Gross Merchandise Value):** Total value of crop sales processed via the platform.
* **Farmer Income Delta:** Average percentage increase in net earnings for onboarded farmers vs. non-platform baseline.
* **Equipment Utilization Rate:** Number of active rental days booked per listed machine.
* **Repeat Buyer Rate:** Percentage of enterprise and domestic buyers returning for multi-season procurement.
